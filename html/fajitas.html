Chicken Fajitas

These easy chicken fajitas are packed with flavor and come together quickly for a perfect weeknight meal. Serve sizzling hot with your favorite toppings!

Mexican Chicken Dinner Quick
Prep: 20 mins
Cook: 20-25 mins
Total: 40-45 mins
Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs boneless, skinless chicken breasts or thighs, thinly sliced
  • 2-3 bell peppers (any color), thinly sliced
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 packet (about 1 oz) fajita seasoning mix (or homemade blend, see below)
  • 1/4 cup water or chicken broth
  • 8-12 flour tortillas (corn tortillas work too)
  • For serving (optional): Salsa, guacamole, sour cream, shredded cheese, lime wedges, fresh cilantro
  • Homemade Fajita Seasoning Blend (if not using a packet):
  • 1 tbsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper (optional, for heat)
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp salt (or to taste)
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

Instructions

  1. Marinate Chicken (optional but recommended): In a bowl, toss the sliced chicken with 1 tbsp of olive oil and half of the fajita seasoning mix. Let it sit for at least 10 minutes, or up to 30 minutes in the refrigerator.
  2. Sauté Vegetables: Heat 1 tbsp of olive oil in a large skillet or cast-iron pan over medium-high heat. Add the sliced bell peppers and onions. Sauté for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until they are tender-crisp and slightly charred. Remove the vegetables from the pan and set aside.
  3. Cook Chicken: Add the remaining 1 tbsp of olive oil to the same skillet. Increase heat to high. Add the seasoned chicken in a single layer (you may need to do this in batches to avoid overcrowding the pan, which steams the chicken instead of searing it). Cook for 3-5 minutes per side, or until browned and cooked through.
  4. Combine and Finish: Return the cooked vegetables to the skillet with the chicken. Sprinkle the remaining fajita seasoning over the chicken and vegetables. Pour in the water or chicken broth. Stir everything together and cook for another 2-3 minutes, allowing the sauce to thicken slightly and coat the ingredients.
  5. Warm Tortillas: While the fajitas are finishing, warm the tortillas. You can do this in a dry skillet over medium heat for about 30 seconds per side, in the microwave (wrapped in a damp paper towel) for 30-60 seconds, or wrapped in foil in a 350°F (175°C) oven for 10-15 minutes.
  6. Serve: Serve the hot chicken and vegetable mixture immediately with the warm tortillas and your favorite toppings.
