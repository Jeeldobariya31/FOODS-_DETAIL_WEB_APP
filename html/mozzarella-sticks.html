Homemade Mozzarella Sticks

Crispy on the outside, gooey on the inside! These homemade mozzarella sticks are a perfect appetizer or snack.

Snack Appetizer Cheesy Fried
Prep: 20 mins
Chill: 1-2 hrs
Cook: 2-3 mins/batch
Serves: 4–6

Ingredients

  • 16 ounces (about 20-25 pieces) part-skim mozzarella string cheese sticks, cut in half
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 2 cups seasoned breadcrumbs (Italian style works well)
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder (optional, for extra flavor in breadcrumbs)
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder (optional)
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper (optional)
  • Oil for frying (vegetable, canola, or peanut oil recommended)
  • Marinara sauce, for serving

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Mozzarella: If using string cheese, cut each stick in half.
  2. Set up Breading Stations: In a shallow dish, place the flour. In a second shallow dish, whisk the eggs. In a third shallow dish, combine the seasoned breadcrumbs with garlic powder, onion powder, and black pepper (if using).
  3. Bread the Mozzarella: Dredge each mozzarella stick in the flour, shaking off any excess. Dip the floured stick into the beaten egg, ensuring it's fully coated. Roll the egg-coated stick in the seasoned breadcrumbs, pressing gently to ensure a thick, even coating.
  4. Double Coat for Best Results: For extra crispiness and to prevent cheese from oozing out during frying, dip the breaded stick back into the egg, then again into the breadcrumbs. This creates a thicker crust.
  5. Chill: Place the breaded mozzarella sticks on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Freeze for at least 1-2 hours, or preferably overnight, until solid. This step is crucial to prevent the cheese from melting too quickly when fried.
  6. Fry: In a large, heavy-bottomed pot or deep fryer, heat 2-3 inches of oil to 350°F (175°C). Use a kitchen thermometer to monitor the temperature. Carefully add a few frozen mozzarella sticks to the hot oil, being careful not to overcrowd the pot. Fry in batches. Fry for 2-3 minutes, or until golden brown and crispy. The cheese inside should be melted and gooey, but not oozing out.
  7. Drain and Serve: Using a slotted spoon, remove the mozzarella sticks from the oil and place them on a plate lined with paper towels to drain excess oil. Serve immediately with warm marinara sauce for dipping.
