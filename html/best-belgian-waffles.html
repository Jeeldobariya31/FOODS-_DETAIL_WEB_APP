The Best Belgian Waffles

Breakfast Crispy Make-Ahead Batter
Prep: 15 mins
Cook: 20 mins
Total: 35 mins
Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 large eggs, separated
  • 1Â¾ cups milk
  • 1/3 cup melted butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla

Instructions

  1. Preheat waffle iron.
  2. Whisk dry ingredients. Mix milk, yolks, butter, vanilla; combine.
  3. Beat egg whites to soft peaks; fold into batter.
  4. Cook waffles until golden and crisp.
