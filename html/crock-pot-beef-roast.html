Crock-Pot Beef Roast

Ultra-tender pot roast with hearty vegetablesâ€”hands-off comfort food.

Recipe | American

Slow CookerBeefComfort

Ingredients

  • 3 lb chuck roast
  • 1 onion, wedged
  • 4 carrots, chopped
  • 3 potatoes, chunked
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 1 tsp rosemary
  • 1 tsp thyme
  • Salt & pepper

Instructions

  1. Season and sear roast on all sides.
  2. Add vegetables to slow cooker; place roast on top.
  3. Pour broth; add herbs. Cook LOW 8 hours.
  4. Rest, slice, and ladle juices over top.
