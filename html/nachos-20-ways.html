Nachos, 20 Ways

From classic sheet-pan nachos to breakfast and dessert twists—20 crowd-pleasing variations.

Recipe | Mexican-Inspired

Party FoodQuick

Ingredients

  • Tortilla chips
  • Shredded cheese
  • Salsa
  • Jalapeños
  • Protein (beans, chicken, beef)
  • Sour cream
  • Guacamole

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Layer chips, cheese, and toppings on a sheet pan.
  3. Bake 6–8 minutes until cheese melts.
  4. Finish with cold toppings and serve hot.
