Copycat McDonald's Big Mac Sauce

That tangy-creamy burger sauce you knowâ€”made easily at home.

Recipe | American

SauceCopycatNo-Cook

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup mayo
  • 2 tbsp relish
  • 1 tbsp mustard
  • 1 tsp white vinegar
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • Pinch garlic & onion powder

Instructions

  1. Combine all ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Chill 30 minutes to meld flavors.
  3. Spread on burgers or use as dip.
