Fluffy Vegan Pancakes

These vegan pancakes are incredibly light, fluffy, and easy to make, perfect for a delicious plant-based breakfast!

Vegan Breakfast Easy Fluffy
Prep: 10 mins
Cook: 15-20 mins
Total: 25-30 mins
Serves: 6-8

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1 ½ cups unsweetened plant-based milk (almond, soy, or oat work well)
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar (or lemon juice)
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil (plus more for greasing the pan)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Prepare the "Buttermilk": In a medium bowl, combine the plant-based milk and apple cider vinegar. Stir gently and let it sit for 5-10 minutes.
  2. Combine Dry Ingredients: In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar.
  3. Combine Wet Ingredients: To the "buttermilk" mixture, add the 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil and vanilla extract. Whisk until well combined.
  4. Combine Wet and Dry: Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients. Stir with a whisk or spatula until just combined. Be careful not to overmix; a few lumps are perfectly fine.
  5. Heat the Pan: Heat a lightly oiled griddle or non-stick frying pan over medium heat.
  6. Cook Pancakes: Pour about ¼ cup of batter per pancake onto the hot griddle. Cook for 2-3 minutes per side, or until golden brown and bubbles appear on the surface.
  7. Serve: Serve immediately with your favorite toppings like fresh fruit, maple syrup, vegan butter, or whipped coconut cream.
