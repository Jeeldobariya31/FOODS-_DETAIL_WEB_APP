50 Mediterranean Diet Recipes You'll Love

A curated set of Mediterranean-inspired dishes emphasizing olive oil, vegetables, grains, and lean proteins.

Recipe | Mediterranean

CollectionHealthyMediterranean

Ingredients

  • Extra-virgin olive oil
  • Fresh tomatoes
  • Cucumbers
  • Feta cheese
  • Olives
  • Whole grains (farro, bulgur)
  • Chickpeas
  • Fresh herbs

Instructions

  1. Choose a base: grains, greens, or legumes.
  2. Add colorful vegetables and a lean protein.
  3. Dress with olive oil, lemon, and herbs.
  4. Top with nuts, seeds, and a sprinkle of cheese.
