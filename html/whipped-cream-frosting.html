Stable Whipped Cream Frosting

This stable whipped cream frosting is perfect for piping on cakes, cupcakes, and desserts. It holds its shape beautifully!

Dessert Frosting Easy Stable
Prep: 10-15 mins
Cook: â€”
Total: 10-15 mins
Serves: Covers 1 cake

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups cold heavy whipping cream

Instructions

  1. Chill Equipment: Chill your mixing bowl and whisk attachment in the freezer for 10-15 minutes.
  2. Whip Cream Cheese: In the chilled bowl, beat the softened cream cheese until smooth and creamy.
  3. Add Sugar & Vanilla: Gradually add the powdered sugar and vanilla extract to the cream cheese, beating until well combined.
  4. Whip Heavy Cream: Slowly pour in the cold heavy whipping cream while continuing to beat on medium-high speed.
  5. Achieve Stiff Peaks: Continue whipping until stiff peaks form. Be careful not to over-whip, or it will become grainy.
  6. Use Immediately: Use the frosting immediately. If storing, keep in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to a day.
